Who are we?

We are a group of passionate 3-D designers that want to make the world into a more creative place.

  • We have been running strong for the past two years, fighting off COVID
  • An amazing set of employees reside here, providing the best products
  • The most amazing designs that our customers have sent us, each one having its own charm

We have been perfecting our business to provide the best experience for those that want to get into the hobby of 3D printing. We receive your 3D models, print them, and ship the final products to you. This allows for those without a printer or who are new to the world of 3D printing to have the ability to create what they have always wanted to.

Why should you choose us?

  • 01 Services Uncompared to Others

    While free-lance options are available to the public, our company strives to make sure that the product that you receive is the way you want it. We are the only online professional 3D printing company that work with the customer to achieve the highest quality products.

  • 02 Ease of use for the general public

    We want 3D printing to be something greater than a hobby. People shouldn't be spending a ton of money on equipment for a hobby that may not last long. Thus, we have developed this company to give the general public some outlet to release their inner creativity.

  • 03 It is a learning process

    Even though people may know some glimpse of the world of 3D printing, not all experience the "coolness" of the art. We want to show people the process of designing a model and proceeding to print that work of art for their own purposes.

 

Frequently Asked Questions

You will find the most popular questions that people have about our company. If you have any other questions, please head over to the contact form and submit a ticket.

  • Where do you ship to? How long does it take to reach me?

    As we are based in New York, we have the capability to ship to anywhere in the United States. While we plan to expand to international shipping, we do not have plans to implement it at the moment. Depending on the size of the model, it may take about 1-4 days in order to create the printed piece(s). This does not include the time it will take to ship the piece.

  • How do you make sure that every piece printed is at the utmost quality?

    We run each printed piece through a series of tests in order to ensure the quality of the print as well as the durability of the piece. If we do not think the piece is satisfactory to your standards and our standards, we will create a new one with no charge.

  • What happens when pieces break during shipping?

    These pieces are made of plastic so breaks during shipping are possible. With this in mind, if you receive broken pieces, you can email us within 5 business days with a complaint and we can ship you a new one. If an email is sent after this deadline, we will not replace the piece.

  • What sizes do you cater to?

    Every size is available to print through EZPRINTS. Smaller prints will come in one piece but larger prints such as a full body print will likely come in multiple pieces.

  • Are the designs limited in any way?

    As long as they do not pertain to the discrimination of race, religion, sexuality, or gender, the designs that you may send can be accepted. Please note that we have the choice to deny any design that you may send to us, whether legal or personal reasons.
